About Us

Udaan MUN is a student-led Model United Nations conference dedicated to fostering diplomacy, critical thinking, and leadership among young minds.

Our journey began with a simple idea

Driven by a passion for diplomacy and student leadership, Udaan MUN was founded with a simple mission to create a meaningful platform for dialogue, debate, and global awareness.

Through carefully curated committees, relevant agendas, and an experienced Executive Board, we aim to deliver an enriching conference that inspires confidence, collaboration, and critical thinking.
